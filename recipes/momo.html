How To Make Momo

Description

Originally from Tibet and Nepal, momos have become a popular street food in India, especially in the northern and northeastern regions. These dumplings are made from thin dough wrappers filled with seasoned vegetables, paneer, or meat and can be steamed, fried, or tossed in spicy sauces. Served with fiery red chutney, momos offer a delicious burst of flavor with every bite.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Cabbage & carrots
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Make the dough : Mix flour and salt, gradually add water, and knead into a smooth dough. Let it rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Make the filling : Heat oil in a pan, saut√© garlic and ginger, then add chopped vegetables, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes and let it cool.
  3. Shape the momos : Roll out the dough thinly, cut into small circles, place a spoonful of filling in the center, and pleat to seal.
  4. Steam : Place momos in a greased steamer and steam for 10-12 minutes until the dough turns translucent.
  5. Serve:: Enjoy with spicy red chutney or soy sauce.
